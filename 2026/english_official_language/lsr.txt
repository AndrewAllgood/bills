1. Short Title.  This Act may be cited as the ``New Hampshire English Language Act of 2026''.
	
2. The Government of New Hampshire shall conduct its official business in English, including publications, forms, web pages, informational materials, face-to-face services, and phone services.

3. The Government of New Hampshire shall not conduct any business in languages other than English, including publications, forms, web pages, informational materials, face-to-face services, and phone services.

4. The state of NH nor any department or administrative unit shall not contract with or pay any person or organization to provide information or services in any language other than English.

5. The state of NH nor any department or administrative unit shall not provide interpreters to anyone except as required under existing judicial rulings regarding due process rights.

6. The state of NH and all departments and administrative units shall allow citizens and others to provide their own interpreters.

7. Exceptions: This chapter does not apply to the use of a language other than English for training in foreign languages.  This bill excepts any federal requirement. 

8. Severability Clause: If any provision of this act or its application is held invalid, the remainder shall not be affected.
